Hearing Systems

About

This website contains materials for the lecture series Hearing Systems, which was held for the first time in winter semester 2020 as part of the Audio Systems Technology course at TU Ilmenau. During the winter semester 2022 it became part of the Audio Signal Processing & Audio Systems course at TU Ilmenau.
